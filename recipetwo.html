How To Prepare Noodle That You Buy For 0,99$

I don't sincerely believe that the noodle you bought from Walmart for 0.99$ will look like this after you cook even if you're Gordon Ramsey but anyway

Ingredients

  • Ready to cook Noodle
  • Seasoning that comes within the packet
  • Boiling Water

    • Steps

    1. Gently take out the ready-to-cook Noodle out of its package and place it in a deep bowl
    2. You can have myriad of combinations of seasoning; thus, add season however you like
    3. Put the bamboo sauce on the noodle block
    4. Pour some boiling water into your bowl and wait for at least 5 minutes
    5. Your Noodle is ready to eat but be mindful about the MSG intake
