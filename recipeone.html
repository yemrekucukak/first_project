How To Prepare Frozen Pizza

Preparing frozen pizza is relatively easy if you're studying engineering. This page is dedicated to show how to prepare frozen pizza

Ingredients

  • One packet of frozen pizza
  • Extra toppings you like

    • Steps

    1. Take the frozen pizza out of its packaging
    2. Add the extra toppings you like on to your pizza
    3. Put it in the microwave
    4. Your pizza will be ready to take out and devour when the cheese is hot and slimy
    5. Dont forget to share it with your room mates
